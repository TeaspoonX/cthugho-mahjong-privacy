Privacy Policy

Cthugho Mahjong

Effective date: 10 May 2026

Overview

Cthugho Mahjong is a single-player Mahjong Solitaire puzzle game for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data Collection

Cthugho Mahjong does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any form of network communication.

Local Storage

The app stores your game progress, settings, and high scores locally on your device using Android's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed automatically if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Third-Party Services

Cthugho Mahjong does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. No data is sent to any external server.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
